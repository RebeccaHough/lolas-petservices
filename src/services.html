Dog Walking

We know that due to busy lives its not always possible to be in two places at once, and leaving your beloved dog can cause stress for both you and them. Now you can rest assured knowing your dog is getting all the care, love and attention they need, whilst you’re not there. Our aim is to be the highlight of your dog’s day!

I’ll collect your pooch and we’ll go for at least an hour’s walk with a small group of up to 4 doggie friends in total. A small group ensures your dog is getting all the attention they need. Walks are stimulating, filled with exercise, fun and play, with plenty of time for sniffing! I can keep you updated with plenty of pics of your dog’s day, and I’ll return your furry friend home safely, towelled down, tired and very happy, ready for a snooze!

We can discuss all your dog’s needs in my free consultation with you, which also includes a free familiarisation walk with you and your dog, so we can get to know each other.

Dog Home Visits

I can visit your dog at home for plenty of fuss, play and letting out into the garden. I can refresh water, feed and administer medication if required.

This service can help if you have a young playful pup, an older pooch or a dog in recovery from illness or operation. Visits will be at least 30 minutes, longer if you require, and made a t a time to suit you and your dog.

Doggie Day Care

Are you out for the day, or working long hours? I can look after your dog for up to 8 hours during this time, if just a walk isn’t enough.

Your dog can join me on walks, meet other furry friends, run and play. I can also feed your dog if required, and make sure they get all the fuss and attention they need. I’ll return your dog home safely, tired and very happy, ready for a good rest!

Cat Home Visits

I can visit your cat at home daily whilst you’re away on holiday. Your feline friend will feel happy and relaxed within their own familiar surroundings. I will fuss and feed your cat, and change litter if required.

I can also clear post, put out bins and water houseplants, giving you peace of mind that not only is your cat happy and cared for, but your home is secure. Visits will be at least 30 minutes at a time to suit you and your cat.

Dog Sitting

Whilst your away from home, or on holiday, I can stay at your house and provide the very best care for your dog.

Your furry friend will feel safe and happy within their own familiar surroundings, with their usual routine undisturbed. Your pooch can join me on walks, meet other doggie friends, and get lots of exercise and play.

We can discuss all your dog’s needs in my free consultation with you, which also includes a free familiarisation walk with you and your dog, so we can get to know each other.

I can also clear post, water house plants, and put out bins, giving you peace of mind that not only is your beloved dog cared for but your home is secure too.
