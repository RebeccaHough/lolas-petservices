Location

Lola’s are based in Rushmoor, and cover the surrounding areas of Tilford, Frensham, Churt, Elstead, Thursley, Beacon Hill, Hindhead and Grayshott.

We are very lucky to have so many wonderful areas to walk on our doorstep! Our adventures often take us to Frensham Ponds, Hankley Common, Thursley Nature Reserve, The Devils Punchbowl and Waggoner’s Wells amongst others.
